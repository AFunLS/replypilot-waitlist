How ReplyPilot works (v1)

  1. Connect your Google Business Profile

    Connect once. ReplyPilot watches for new reviews.

  2. Apply "Star Rules" (guardrails you can trust)

    4-5★: auto-reply (or auto-draft). 3★ and below: draft-only + escalation.

  3. Brand voice, not generic AI

    Use your website + existing replies + do/don't rules (forbidden phrases, promo policy).

  4. Crisis mode for bad reviews

    Alerts + 3 suggested replies + a short internal checklist (refund? replacement? outreach?).

FAQ

Does ReplyPilot auto-post replies?

Only if you turn it on - and only for reviews that match your safe rules (typically 4-5★). Negative reviews default to draft-only + escalation.

What if the AI writes something off-brand or risky?

You control tone, max length, forbidden phrases, and promo/discount policy. You can keep everything in draft-only mode.

Is this for Google only?

V1 focuses on Google Business Profile because it's where most SMB review volume lives. Other platforms may come later.

Can I use this for multiple locations?

Yes. Pricing is per location and the inbox supports multi-location views.

How long does setup take?

Target: 10 minutes.

Is there a free trial?

For early access, we'll offer a short trial (or a "first month refundable") depending on onboarding load.
